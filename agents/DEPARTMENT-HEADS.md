## How to use this page

You don't need to remember the specialist names. Just talk to the Department Head:

> "Luca, the 1PM landing page is broken in mobile."

Luca decides whether it's a frontend issue (Beni), a CSS issue (Beni), a build issue (Moshe), or a regression (Bruno) — and routes accordingly.

Even simpler:

> "Massimo, the 1PM landing page is broken in mobile."

Massimo routes to Luca, who routes to the right specialist.
